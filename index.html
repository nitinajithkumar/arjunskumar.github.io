About Me

Hi, I'm Arjun graduated from Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Amrita School of Engineering, Amritapuri Campus, India with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science & Engineering and Master's degree in Robotics & Automation. I have experience working with Robotics company in Portugal and India as well as top MNC firm such as Ernst and Young (EY), India.

I'm very passionate about the applications of computer vision and deep learning in robotics: mobile robots and autonomous vehicles in particular. My interests lie in Computer vision, Deep learning, Artificial Intelligence, Augmented Reality, Path planning, Robot autonomy and Product development. I intend to enhance human-robot interaction through my research and design robotic systems that augment the quality of human lives.
